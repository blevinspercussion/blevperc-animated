I'm Adam, a professional drummer, percussionist, and educator residing in Chattanooga, TN. I am currently available for gigs, private lessons, group lessons, clinics, etc.

Live Drums/Percussion

With over 20 years of professional experience as a live and studio drummer, I offer top-quality beats and rhythms for your performances or recordings. I have established a reputation for excellence, versatility, and creativity in the industry.

Whether you need a dynamic, energetic beat for a live gig or a perfectly timed groove for a studio recording, I have the skills and expertise to make it happen. Browse my website to learn more about my services, experience, and portfolio. Contact me today to discuss your project and take your music to the next level!

I am well-versed in a wide variety of musical genres, having performed professionally in the styles of rock, pop, jazz, country, orchestra, latin/world music, ska/reggae, and more! I am also a skilled percussionist and I am qualified to play virtually any percussion instrument, including hand drums (congas, bongos, cajon, etc.), mallet percussion (vibraphone, xylophone, marimba, etc.), orchestral and rudimental snare, drumset, and much, much more.

Education

I have been an active music educator since I took on my first private student in 1996. Since that time I have taught hundreds of students through private lessons, group classes and marching drumline instruction.

I have been a staff instructor at the two premier private music schools in Birmingham, AL as well as over a dozen private schools throughout Chattanooga, Birmingham, and North Georgia. I currently teach at Lakeview Middle School in North Georgia and East Hamilton Middle School and Ooltewah High School both in Hamilton County, TN.

My background in classical percussion and rudimental snare drum, as well as my study of contemporary drumset techniques, ensures that I have the knowledge to help each and every student reach their individual goals.

Whether you'd like to play in a professional band, study in a collegiate music program, or just jam with your friends I can teach you the skills necessary to do so to the best of your ability! Contact me to inquire about private lessons or a for group instruction at a school or for a one-time clinic or masterclass.
